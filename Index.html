Services

Web design and development

Sazu builds and takes care of your new website. We provide perfect cooperation with our customers and strive to the ease and comfort of the user. Sazu makes software that simplifies and clarifies the administration of your company. If you choose Sazu, you choose more time for the real work. We are improving every day in order to achieve new web projects and existing websites. In both designing and developing websites we're always looking for more values to your project as just that extra. Whether it's a simple website or an extensive shop or communty, we make it as user-friendly as we can.

Web strategy

Having a website is not enough to make a success of your Internet presence. In a web strategy we determine the goals you want to achieve and we will establish a plan whereby we address all the necessary Internet channels to work towards these goals. A solid web strategy is a mix of the use of social media, search engine optimization, marketing , email marketing and your website itself. This mix will lead you to your desired goals.

Email marketing

E- mail is a very important way of communication and not to imagine in today's professional life. Email marketing should be one of the pillars of your web strategy. A thoughtful email marketing strategy is great value for your readers and is a very effective element of your overall online strategy.
